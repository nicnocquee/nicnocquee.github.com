[iPad] Upgraded to iOS 6 for the Magazine

written in ios, ipad, share

Marco Ament on the Foreword of The Magazine:

Many iPad magazines are carrying unnecessary and expensive baggage from their print days. Some born-digital magazines even took on print baggage simply because they thought they needed it.

Indeed.

With many problems like the wifi issue, I have been hesitant to upgrade my iPad to iOS 6. But The Magazine is approved by John Grubber. And concurred by MG Siegler. A week free trial. Furthermore, I like reading articles. And this one looks slick. But it needs iOS 6. There, I installed it. Let’s see if it’s worth it. You can download The Magazine from the App Store.
